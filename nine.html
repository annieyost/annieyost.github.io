Nine Magazine

In Typography III, all Graphic Design students have to design a magazine. The magazine can be on any topic. I decided to design a Women's sports magazine, because I was sick of seeing so many men on Sports magazines when there are so many badass female athletes.

Spreads

Below are the magazine spreads for two seperate articles; one about rockclimbing and the other about the women's soccer team fight for equal pay. Typefaces are Sharp Grotesk for Headlines and Publico for text.
